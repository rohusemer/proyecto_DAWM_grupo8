Our History

Founded in 2021, MoneyTracker established itself as a financial icon in Ecuador. With its unique brand of personal finance you can't find it anywhere else. With four of the best students from the best university in Ecuador, you can have all your finances in one place.

MoneyTracker has its origins as a project of the Web and Mobile Development course at ESPOL, taught by Ing. Allan Avendaño.
